Who can use the website?

The website is exclusively tailored for Jamaican MSMEs, including startups and established businesses, seeking to gain valuable insights into market trends, customer preferences, and competitive analyses. Whether you are a product-based or service-based business, the platform provides tools to assist you in evaluating demand and refining your business approach.

How can the website help my business grow?

By using the demand assessment tools, you can identify market opportunities, understand customer needs, and tailor your offerings accordingly. The market intelligence section provides valuable insights to keep you updated with current trends, while the competitive analysis enables you to identify key competitors and make informed decisions to stand out in the market.

Is the website available to all Jamaican businesses?

Yes, the website is open to all Jamaican MSMEs, regardless of the industry or sector they operate in.

Is there a cost associated with using the website?

The website operates on a freemium model, offering general market research information and demand assessment resources for free to all users. To gain access to our basic market research tools and demand assessment features, an active NCB account is required. Users can log in to the website sing their NCB credentials to verify eligibility for these basic tools.

For those seeking more comprehensive insights and advanced functionalities, we offer a premium tier, which includes access to our business diagnostic tool and a range of more sophisticated market research and demand assessment tools. The pricing details for the premium tier can be found on the website.

How often is the website updated with new insights?

The website's resources are regularly updated every month to ensure that businesses have access to the latest trends, data, and tools required for successful decision-making.
