What is GLANCE Analytics?

GLANCE Analytics is a comprehensive API that aggregates and analyzes essential data points crucial for understanding Jamaica's market dynamics. It harnesses the power of consumer spending habits, small business statistics, parish census information, local and international stock data, and annual industry performance reports, all in one integrated platform.
